How to run a review in Shodh

Eight steps, from an empty desk to an exported PRISMA diagram. The walkthrough plays on its own — pause any time, or jump to the step you need. Everything shown here happens on your own machine; nothing is uploaded.

01 Desk

Create the review

Give the review a title and state the question it answers. Your name goes on every decision you record, so the audit trail says who did what.

You can run several reviews side by side. Each keeps its own records, decisions, and PDFs.
02 Import

Import your search results

Export your database searches as RIS, BibTeX, or CSV and drop the files in. Scopus, Web of Science, PubMed, Zotero, Mendeley, and EndNote all produce at least one of these.

Drop PDFs in too. Text is extracted at import so it can be searched later in Ask. Scanned PDFs have no text layer — Shodh does not run OCR, so run those through an OCR tool first if you need them searchable.
03 Duplicates

Remove duplicates

Searching several databases returns the same paper more than once. Shodh finds candidates in three passes: identical DOI, identical title, then close-but-not-identical title.

Exact DOI
Prediction of flank wear in dry turning using force signals
10.0000/example.1 · matched 2 records
Fuzzy title · 94%
Neural network modelling of surface roughness in end milling
American / British spelling variant
Check before you remove. Fuzzy matching is a heuristic — it will miss some duplicates and flag some records that are not duplicates. Shodh never deletes anything on its own; you confirm each one, and every removal is written to the decision log.
04 Stage 1

Title screening

Work through the records on title, authors, and journal alone. One keystroke per decision. Anything you include moves forward to full text.

record · 2021 · screening as R. Sharma
Prediction of flank wear in dry turning of stainless steel using cutting force signals
Doe, J.; Roe, A. · Journal of Manufacturing Science · 2021
Include→ or I
Exclude← or E
MaybeM
Pressed the wrong key? U undoes the last decision. Abstracts deliberately stay hidden until Stage 2 — this keeps the first pass fast and consistent.
05 Conflicts

Resolve conflicts

When two reviewers screen the same records, disagreements collect here for adjudication. Each reviewer's vote stays hidden from the other until the record is resolved.

Wavelet analysis of accelerometer signals for chatter detection
R. Sharma Include   A. Kovács Exclude
Include
Exclude
Independence is by convention, not enforced. Shodh has no server, so it cannot stop two reviewers sharing one browser profile. Screen on separate machines or separate profiles, and describe your procedure in your methods section.
06 Stage 2

Full-text screening

Records that passed Stage 1 arrive here with their abstracts and any attached PDF. Decide one by one, or write a keyword rule and apply it to the whole matched set at once.

Comparison of stamping and hydroforming for bipolar plates
Wells, P. · 2022 · PDF attached
Stamping, hydroforming, and machining routes are compared on cost, cycle time, and channel accuracy…
KEYWORD RULE
hydroforming OR stamping
matches 14 records
Include set
Exclude set
Every exclusion at this stage needs a PRISMA reason — wrong population, wrong outcome, and so on. That is what fills the exclusion boxes in your flow diagram.
07 Ask

Search what you have collected

Ask searches the abstracts and extracted PDF text of the records you imported, and returns the passages that match, ranked by relevance.

QUERY
How can we help the environment with sustainable manufacturing?
rank 1 · book · 2024
Sustainability in Smart Manufacturing: Trends, Scope, and Challenges
Shah, S.; Nautiyal, H.; Gugliani, G.; Kumar, A.; Namboodri, T.; Singla, Y. K. (Eds.) · CRC Press · doi 10.1201/9781003467496
…covers 3D printing and rapid prototyping for low-carbon development, and the role of AI in digital manufacturing…
rank 2 · chapter · pp. 1–18
Globalization and International Issues in Sustainable Manufacturing
Kumar, A.; Singla, Y. K.; Namboodri, T. · doi 10.1201/9781003467496-1
…the interaction between global supply chains and sustainable manufacturing practice…
This is keyword search, not AI comprehension. Ask ranks passages by BM25, a classical word-matching method — it will miss relevant text that uses different vocabulary. If you install Ollama separately, retrieved passages can optionally be sent to a local model for a draft synthesis; that stays on your machine, and anything it writes must be checked against the sources.

Records shown are real and correctly cited; the highlighted excerpts are illustrative of how results are displayed, not verbatim quotations.

08 Export

Two things to do before you publish. Verify the PRISMA counts against your own records — they reflect what you did in the app, but the numbers in your paper are your responsibility. And export a JSON backup regularly: your review lives in this browser profile only, so clearing browser data or switching machines without a backup loses it.
